Referral Program Terms

SETOR AI SP. Z O.O. - Referral Partner Program Terms - Effective Date: March 2026 - Draft for legal review

1. Definitions

"Affiliate" or "Partner" refers to any individual or entity accepted into the Setor AI Referral Partner Program. "Referral" means a new Setor AI customer who signs up using the Partner's unique referral link. "Active Referral" means a Referral with a current, paid Setor AI subscription. "Commission" means the percentage-based payment earned by the Partner for each Active Referral's subscription payment. "Draw Entry" means one entry into a promotional prize draw, earned per Active Referral at the time of the draw.

2. Eligibility and Enrollment

The Program is open to individuals and entities approved by Setor AI. Enrollment requires a valid email address and acceptance of these terms. Setor AI reserves the right to approve, reject, or suspend any Partner at its sole discretion. Self-referrals are prohibited.

3. Commission Structure

Partners earn a lifetime commission on each Active Referral's monthly subscription payment. The commission rate is determined by the Partner's active referral count at the time the Referral signs up: 10% (1-2 referrals), 15% (3-5), 25% (6-10), 35% (11+). Rates are locked per Referral at signup and do not change retroactively. Tier 2 commissions of 5% are earned on subscriptions from referrals brought by the Partner's own referrals, limited to one level.

4. First Referral Bonus

A one-time $500 bonus is credited to the Partner's balance when their first Referral's first payment has been active for 30 consecutive days without refund. If the payment is refunded after the bonus is credited, a clawback of $500 is applied to the Partner's balance.

5. Payment Terms

Commissions are credited to the Partner's balance 30 days after the referred client's payment, to allow for refund and chargeback processing. For Partners who are also Setor AI subscribers, credits may be automatically applied to the next subscription invoice. External Partners may request a bank transfer payout with a minimum of $100, subject to submission of a valid invoice and administrative approval. Only one pending payout request is permitted at a time.

6. Physical Rewards

Physical rewards (including but not limited to Oura Ring 4, Rolex Datejust 41mm) are awarded at the sole discretion of Setor AI after the Partner reaches and maintains the required tier for 90 consecutive days. Rewards are subject to availability and may be substituted with an equivalent value item. Fulfillment is arranged personally by the founding team.

7. Draw Rewards

Promotional draws (including but not limited to Forestis Dolomites stay, Acquisition.com Vegas Workshop) award one entry per Active Referral at the time of the draw. Winners are selected randomly and announced via email and WhatsApp on the dates published on this page. Setor AI reserves the right to modify draw dates, prizes, or cancel draws with reasonable notice.

8. Grace Period and Deactivation

When a Referral cancels their subscription, a 45-day grace period begins. If the Referral resubscribes within this period, the referral relationship is reactivated automatically. After 45 days, the Referral is deactivated and no longer counts as an Active Referral.

9. Prohibited Activities

Partners shall not: (a) make false or misleading claims about Setor AI; (b) use spam, unsolicited messages, or deceptive practices to generate referrals; (c) create fake accounts or self-refer; (d) bid on Setor AI branded keywords in paid advertising without prior written consent; (e) violate any applicable law or regulation in their promotional activities.

10. Modification and Termination

Setor AI reserves the right to modify these terms, commission rates, reward offerings, or the Program itself at any time with 30 days notice to active Partners. Setor AI may suspend or terminate a Partner's participation for violation of these terms or for any reason with written notice. Upon termination, the Partner's unpaid balance above $100 will be paid out within 60 days.

11. Limitation of Liability

Setor AI's total liability to any Partner shall not exceed the total commissions actually paid to that Partner in the preceding 12 months. Setor AI is not liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages arising from the Partner's participation in the Program.

12. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Poland. Any disputes shall be resolved by the competent courts in Lublin, Poland. The Program is operated by SETOR AI SP. Z O.O., NIP: PL7123502599, registered in Poland.
